About Me

I am Tayyaba Arooj, an AI researcher with a strong focus on applying artificial intelligence to healthcare and socially impactful domains. My work lies at the intersection of machine learning, deep learning, and medical data analysis, with an emphasis on building reliable, interpretable, and deployable AI systems. I am particularly interested in advancing trustworthy NLP, large language model (LLM) reasoning, and AI-based clinical decision support tools. Through my research, I aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI methods and real-world healthcare challenges, especially in resource-constrained settings.

Research Interests

  • AI for Healthcare and Medical Image Analysis
  • Trustworthy and Ethical NLP Systems
  • Large Language Models (LLMs) and Reasoning
  • Explainable and Reliable AI
  • AI-based Clinical Decision Support Tools
